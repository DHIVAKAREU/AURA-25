About AURA '25

AURA - Advancement in Unique Research and Approaches is a prestigious National Level Event organized by our institution. This event brings together the brightest minds from diverse fields to showcase pioneering ideas and groundbreaking research. AURA is a platform for fostering innovation, collaboration, and critical thinking, inviting participants to present cutting-edge solutions to modern challenges.

Travel Information

By Air

The nearest airport is Coimbatore International Airport (CJB), approximately 70km away. Taxis and buses are readily available from the airport.

By Train

Erode Junction (ED) is the closest major railway station, around 60km from the campus. You can hire a cab or take a bus to Sathyamangalam.

By Road

The campus is well-connected by road. Sathyamangalam bus stand is just 5km away, with frequent services from Erode, Coimbatore, and other major cities.
